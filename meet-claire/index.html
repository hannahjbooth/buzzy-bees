Meet Claire

All the Chartered Physiotherapists at Buzzy Bees Physiotherapy are HCPC and CSP registered.

Claire WilliamsonBSc Hons, MCSP, HCPC

Buzzy Bees Director and Chartered Physiotherapist

I qualified as a Physiotherapist in 2014 from the University of Birmingham.

I love all aspects being a physiotherapist has to offer, and my particular passion is paediatrics and elderly rehabilitation. I have gained experience working in many NHS physiotherapy specialities and quickly realised that working with children in paediatrics, adult and elderly rehab were my particular passion.

I love seeing clients of all ages improve and being able to be a part of their journey to health and recovery, whether that be in their own home, nursing home, school or community setting.

In my spare time I like to walk my dog and take my baby swimming or to the park.

Professional registrations include:

  • BSc Hons - University of Birmingham 2014
  • Health Care Professions Council (HCPC) - PH104255
  • Chartered Society of Physiotherapy (CSP) - 096230
  • Association of Paediatric Chartered Physiotherapists
  • Aquatic Therapy Association of Chartered Physiotherapists
