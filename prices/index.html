Baby/child MOT

Do you want peace of mind that your baby or child is developing typically or do you have a niggle of concern and want an expert opinion?

Our experienced children’s physiotherapist will complete a thorough assessment, answer questions and provide advice on how to best to support your baby’s development.

No question is too big or small.

We specialise in:

  • Gross Motor Skill development (rolling, sitting, crawling, walking, running, jumping etc)
  • Milestones
  • Muscle tightness
  • Muscle strength
  • Head turn preference
  • Flat or rolling feet, turning feet or toe walking

Price:

£90 for up to 1 hour assessment

£75 for up to 45 minute follow up appointment

Baby Massage

Suitable for babies from 4 weeks old to crawling.

Baby massage is a wonderful way to bond with your baby. Research shows that routine skin-to-skin contact by a parent or loving caregiver supports your baby’s growth, communication and learning (Royal Surrey NHS Foundation Trust).

Each session lasts between 30-40 minutes and will depend on your baby. Over 5 sessions our team will teach you techniques to try to alleviate issue like wind, colic and teething pain. As well as providing guidance on the right massage oils for baby’s skin and massage technique handouts.
