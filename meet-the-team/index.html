Meet the Team

All the Chartered Physiotherapists at Buzzy Bees Physiotherapy are HCPC and CSP registered.

Claire WilliamsonBSc Hons, MCSP, HCPC

Buzzy Bees Director and Chartered Physiotherapist

I qualified as a Chartered Physiotherapist in 2014 from the University of Birmingham.

I love all aspects being a physiotherapist has to offer, and my particular passion is paediatrics and elderly rehabilitation. I have gained experience working in many NHS physiotherapy specialities and quickly realised that working with children in paediatrics, adult and elderly rehab were my particular passion.

I love seeing clients of all ages improve and being able to be a part of their journey to health and recovery, whether that be in their own home, nursing home, school or community setting.

In my spare time I like to walk my dog and take my baby swimming or to the park.

Professional registrations include:

  • BSc Hons - University of Birmingham 2014
  • Health Care Professions Council (HCPC) - PH104255
  • Chartered Society of Physiotherapy (CSP) - 096230
  • Association of Paediatric Chartered Physiotherapists
  • Aquatic Therapy Association of Chartered Physiotherapists

Lauren BottBSc Hons, MCSP, HCPC

Chartered Physiotherapist

I qualified as a Chartered Physiotherapist in 2016 from Kings College London.

After I qualified I moved back home to the Midlands where I have gained lots of experience working as a physio in both hospital and community settings. I have over 8 years of experience working with children and young adults in multiple specialties including neurology, oncology, musculoskeletal, rheumatology, orthopaedics and rehabilitation. It brings me so much joy helping young people to achieve their goals and reach their optimal potential. It’s a real privilege to be able to be part of my clients rehab journeys.

In my spare time I like to be outside running, cycling or hiking in beautiful places. I love training for ultra marathons and seeing what the human body is capable of.

Professional registrations include:

  • BSc Hons - Kings College London 2016
  • Health Care Professions Council (HCPC) - PH109567
  • Chartered Society of Physiotherapy (CSP) member
  • Association of Paediatric Chartered Physiotherapists member

Molly WadeBSc Hons, MCSP, HCPC

Chartered Physiotherapist

I qualified as a Chartered Physiotherapist in 2021 from the University of Coventry.

I love working as a physiotherapist and have gained a wide range of experience during my time rotating in the NHS. I enjoy seeing people achieve their goals and have a better quality of life. I also work for the NHS on Elderly and Medical wards, so have a good understanding of community services available to also help with rehabilitation and support.

Expertise:

  • Elderly rehab
  • Post hospital discharge rehabilitation
  • Falls prevention and rehabilitation
  • Mobility and balance problems

I enjoy watching a range of sports and spend most weekends watching Formula 1. I regularly play badminton and have been reconnecting with my love of running. I enjoy travelling and exploring new places, so love hearing people's own travelling stories!

Professional registrations include:

  • BSc Hons - University of Coventry 2021
  • Health Care Professions Council (HCPC) - PH125690
  • Chartered Society of Physiotherapy (CSP) - 114665
